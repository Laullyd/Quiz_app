1. Neither the manager nor the employees ______ satisfied with the new policy.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, When using neither…nor, the verb agrees with the subject closest to it (employees → plural), so “are”/ is correct.

2. Hardly had she entered the room ______ the phone rang.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, The structure is “Hardly…when”, a standard inversion expression for past events, → when.

3. If I ______ about the meeting earlier, I would have attended.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, This is a third conditional (past unreal). If + past perfect → would have + past participle → had known.

4. The book, along with several articles, ______ placed on the table.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, The main subject is book (singular). “along with…” does not affect agreement → was.

5. No sooner had they arrived ______ it started raining.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, The correct correlative structure is “No sooner…than”.

6. Each of the players ______ responsible for their own equipment.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Each is singular, so it takes a singular verb → is.

7. She insisted that he ______ immediately.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, After verbs like insist, we use the subjunctive mood. Insisted that he leave (base verb).

8. I would rather you ______ me the truth yesterday.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Would rather + subject + past perfect expresses regret about the past → had told.

9. Not only ______ late, but he also forgot his documents.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, After Not only, we use inversion. Auxiliary verb comes before the subject → was he.

10. The teacher, as well as the students, ______ excited about the trip.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, The subject is teacher (singular). “as well as…” does not change the number → is.

11. Had I known the truth, I ______ differently.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Inverted third conditional. Had I known = If I had known → result is would have acted.

12. She has been working here ______ five years.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, For is used with a duration (five years), while since is for a starting point → for.

13. The committee ______ divided in their opinions.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Committee can be treated as plural when members act individually → are.

14. He behaves as though he ______ everything.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, After as though, we use past tense for unreal situations → knew.

15. By the time we arrived, they ______ dinner.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Past perfect shows an action completed before another past action → had finished.

16. I wish I ______ taller.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, In wishes about unreal present situations, we use the subjunctive “were” for all subjects → were.

17. Scarcely ______ the announcement made when the crowd reacted.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Correct inversion: Scarcely had…when → requires past perfect (had been).

18. There ______ a number of issues to consider.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, A number of = plural → takes a plural verb (are).

19. He is one of the students who ______ always punctual.
You picked: .
Meanwhile, Who refers to students (plural), so the verb must be are.
